June 4th, 2025 Announcement

AetherX Rewrite Announcement

We're making major changes to ensure AetherX's future stability. Here's what you need to know.

Read full post

This wasn't an easy decision, but it's necessary for AetherX's long-term health.

Why We're Rewriting

  • Version Conflicts - The bot was running two different codebases simultaneously
  • Bug Accumulation - Months of patches created unstable spaghetti code
  • Database Issues - Frequent lockups made features unreliable

What This Means

Temporary Changes:

  • Dabloons Reset - The old database is retired (all balances cleared)
  • Feature Availability - Some commands temporarily disabled

Future Improvements:

  • Fresh Start - Everyone will receive 500,000 Dabloons when the new database launches
  • Reliability - New infrastructure to prevent lockups
  • Feature Preservation - All your favorite commands will return

Development Timeline

This will be a slow, careful process. We'll post regular updates about:

  • Completed feature migrations
  • Database progress
  • Upcoming changes

Our Promise to You

We're committed to maintaining AetherX's spirit while fixing its foundation. Some implementations may change as we discover better solutions during the rewrite.

Thank you for your patience during this transition. The result will be worth the wait.

- The AetherX Development Team

Gambler's Paradise Has Arrived!

We're excited to finally bring you the update you (might) have been waiting for!

Read full post

After extensive testing and development, we're proud to release one of our biggest updates yet!

New Features

Here's what's new in the Gambler's Paradise update:

Casino Minigames:

  • Blackjack - Try your luck against the dealer
  • Slots - Spin to win with our colorful slot machine
  • Highlow - Guess if the next number will be higher or lower

Currency System:

All games now use our new Dabloons currency system. Earn them, spend them, and try not to lose them all!

  • Completely reworked $profile command

As promised in our last update, we've added the streak multiplier to Highlow! Each correct guess increases your multiplier (up to 5x), giving you bigger rewards until you guess incorrectly.
